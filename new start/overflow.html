The famous quote "Now I am become Death, the destroyer of worlds" is attributed to J. Robert Oppenheimer, an American physicist and one of the key figures in the development of the atomic bomb. He reportedly said this quote after witnessing the first successful test of a nuclear bomb, the Trinity test, on July 16, 1945, as part of the Manhattan Project during World War II. Cristiano Ronaldo, the renowned Portuguese footballer, has said many inspirational and motivational quotes throughout his career. One of his well-known quotes is: "Your love makes me strong, your hate makes me unstoppable."
